About Dancing Crane

Dancing Crane is the oldest Georgian cultural center and repertory theater company in New York. They offer master classes in traditional Georgian dance, music, and theater for students of all ages, and raise awareness of these traditions through world-class performances throughout the five boroughs of New York. They address the needs of a growing Georgian immigrant community, offering educational programs for families and children, and providing a space for professional artists to practice and elevate their work.

Find out more at dancingcrane.org

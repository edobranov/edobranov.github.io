About GORANA DANCE

GORANA DANCE is a Bulgarian folk dance collective founded in 2006 by Elissaveta Iordanova. The collective's style is characterized by lively energy, swift steps and exciting interpretation of the traditional Bulgarian folk dance form.

Its mission is presenting Bulgarian folk dance art, making connections with communities beyond the Bulgarian one, collaborating with Bulgarian & intercultural folk artists, and serving as a cultural ambassador while being a vital part of the multicultural tapestry of NYC.

GORANA DANCE has been presenting annual concerts for 15+ years and performing on different occassions like festivals in the greater NYC, New Jersey, Pennsylvania and Chicago areas.

Elissaveta Iordanova

Elissaveta is a dance artist and choreographer with professional experience, long-lasting interest & specialty in Bulgarian folk dance, and in-depth knowledge of Bulgarian folkloric heritage.

She is the founder, artistic director and choreographer of the GORANA DANCE Collective, uniting several generations of Bulgarian and American dancers around the captivating power of traditional folklore music & dance.

Elissaveta is a recipient of LMCC grants and her collective has also been supported by the Tienaderrah Foundation and private donors.

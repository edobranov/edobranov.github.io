About Algara Dance

Algara Dance is a Bulgarian folk dance collective founded in 2018 by Galina Saharova and a group of enthusiastic Bulgarian women living in New Jersey. The group's goal is to present their appreciation of Bulgarian folk traditions to larger audiences.

Galina began dancing in Bulgaria in her adolescent years and performed in numerous concerts and festivals with the "Gadularche" company. She is the choreographer of Algara's repertoire as well as a dancer in both Algara Dance and Gorana Dance.

Horoteka Nights

GORANA DANCE is hosting a horoteka series! These are guided dance nights open to dancers of all levels and backgrounds. Come join us for a fun night of unique Bulgarian folk dances and community, even if you're brand new!

Please bring $25 in cash for admission and comfortable shoes. Light snacks and refreshments are provided.

Dates:

Horotekas are on the last or second to last Friday of every month.
